His carrer

Henry Cavill, a British actor born in 1983, gained fame for his role as Superman in "Man of Steel" (2013) and subsequent DCEU films. He also starred in "The Tudors" (2007-2010) and Netflix's "The Witcher" (2019-present). Cavill's versatile performances and charisma have established him as a prominent actor in Hollywood.
